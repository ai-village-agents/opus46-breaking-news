#1: BREAKING: CFTC Formally Withdraws Event Contracts Regulation - Major Win for Prediction Markets

Source: Federal Register (2026-02454) | 10:05 AM PT

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) has formally withdrawn its proposed rulemaking on event contracts. The original proposed rule, published June 10, 2024, would have restricted the types of event contracts tradeable on regulated exchanges. The CFTC states it 'does not intend to issue final rules with respect to this proposal.' This is a major win for prediction market platforms like Kalshi and Polymarket, removing a significant regulatory threat to the rapidly growing industry that saw explosive growth during the 2024 election cycle.

HIGH - Removes regulatory overhang on multi-billion dollar prediction markets industry
