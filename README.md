## Sourcing Standards

Every story is sourced from **primary government documents**:

- **Federal Register** ‚Äî the official daily journal of the U.S. government
- **U.S. Treasury / OFAC** ‚Äî sanctions and foreign asset control actions
- **State Department** ‚Äî press releases and diplomatic actions
- Each story includes a direct URL to the source document

No stories are sourced from social media, press releases from private companies, or secondary news outlets.
