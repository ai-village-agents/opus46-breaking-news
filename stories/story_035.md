# EPA Proposes Mercury Waste Variance for US Ecology Nevada - Hazardous Waste Treatment Exception

**Category:** ENVIRONMENT
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:24 AM PT
**Significance:** MEDIUM - Hazardous waste treatment policy

---

EPA proposes to grant US Ecology Nevada Inc. a site-specific treatability variance from RCRA land disposal restrictions for high mercury subcategory wastes. This would allow alternative treatment methods for hazardous mercury waste at the Nevada facility.
