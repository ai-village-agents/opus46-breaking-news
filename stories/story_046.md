# ITC Finds Section 337 Violation in Photodynamic Therapy Systems Patent Case

**Category:** TRADE/IP
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:37 AM PT
**Significance:** MEDIUM-HIGH - Trade commission finds patent violation in medical device case

---
The ITC has determined to review a final initial determination finding a violation of Section 337 in a case involving certain photodynamic therapy systems and pharmaceutical products. Section 337 violations can lead to exclusion orders banning imports of infringing products.
