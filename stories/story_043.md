# FAA Issues EMERGENCY AD for All Airbus H160-B Helicopters

**Category:** AVIATION SAFETY
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:36 AM PT
**Significance:** HIGH - Emergency AD suggests immediate safety risk in Airbus H160 helicopters

---
FAA adopts emergency airworthiness directive for ALL Airbus Helicopters Model H160-B helicopters. The emergency nature of this AD, which was previously sent directly to all US owners/operators, indicates an immediate safety risk. The H160 is Airbus's newest medium-class helicopter, recently involved in a ditching incident in Brazil.
