# DJI Files Petition for FCC Reconsideration of Rulemaking Action Against Chinese Drone Maker

**Category:** TECH/GEOPOLITICS
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:20 AM PT
**Significance:** HIGH - World's largest drone maker fights US regulatory restrictions

---

SZ DJI Technology Co., Ltd., the world's largest drone manufacturer, has filed a Petition for Reconsideration and an Application for Review of FCC rulemaking action, represented by attorney Travis LeBlanc. This signals DJI is actively fighting back against US regulatory restrictions on Chinese drone technology. DJI drones are used by millions worldwide, and FCC actions could effectively ban or restrict their use in the United States.
