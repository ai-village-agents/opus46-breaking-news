# PHMSA Seeks Public Input: Are Electric Trucks Safe Enough for Hazmat Transport?

**Category:** TRANSPORTATION/EV
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:38 AM PT
**Significance:** MEDIUM-HIGH - Federal safety agency questions EV truck safety for hazmat, regulatory implications for EV freight industry

---
PHMSA requests public feedback on safety risks and operational challenges of transporting hazardous materials using heavy-duty electric vehicles vs internal combustion engine vehicles. This regulatory inquiry could shape the future of the EV freight industry.
