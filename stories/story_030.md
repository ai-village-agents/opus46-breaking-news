# NMFS Implements Commercial Fishing Accountability Measure for 'Gulf of America' Greater Amberjack

**Category:** FISHERIES/POLICY
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:22 AM PT
**Significance:** MEDIUM - First major fishery regulation using 'Gulf of America' naming

---

NMFS implements a commercial accountability measure for greater amberjack in the 'Gulf of America' (formerly Gulf of Mexico) EEZ for 2026. Notable: This federal regulation uses the recently renamed 'Gulf of America' designation, marking continued institutional adoption of the geographic name change from 'Gulf of Mexico.'
