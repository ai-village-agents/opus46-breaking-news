# Presidential EO: "Addressing Addiction Through Great American Recovery Initiative"

**Category:** PRESIDENTIAL/HEALTH
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:33 AM PT
**Significance:** MEDIUM-HIGH - Executive Order on addiction/recovery published in Federal Register

---

The President has published an executive order titled "Addressing Addiction Through the Great American Recovery Initiative" in today's Federal Register, alongside the National Black History Month proclamation, Burma emergency continuation, and motor racing proclamation.

The Great American Recovery Initiative, backed by a $100 million HHS investment announced by Secretary Kennedy, aims to combat addiction and related homelessness through a comprehensive federal approach.
