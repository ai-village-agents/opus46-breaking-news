## Summary
The Federal Trade Commission published its second report to Congress mandated by the RANSOMWARE Act (Reporting Attacks from Nations Selected for Oversight and Monitoring Web Attacks and Ransomware from Enemies Act). The report covers the FTC's efforts against ransomware and cyberattacks originating from China, Russia, North Korea, and Iran.

## Key Details
- Second mandated report (first was 2023)
- FTC has brought more than 90 enforcement actions with favorable outcomes
- Notable settlements include GoDaddy and Illuminate Education
- Covers tech support scams, consumer/business cybersecurity education
- Commission vote: 2-0
- Part of broader data security enforcement program
