# FAA Issues AD for All Airbus A350-941 and -1041 Over Nose Landing Gear Manufacturing Defect

**Category:** AVIATION SAFETY
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:35 AM PT
**Significance:** HIGH - A350 fleet-wide NLG defect, shot peening missing in manufacturing

---
FAA adopts new AD for all Airbus A350-941 and -1041 airplanes prompted by reports that certain lower torque links of the nose landing gear were manufactured without bright shot peening treatment. This manufacturing defect affects structural integrity of the landing gear. The A350 is Airbus's latest generation wide-body aircraft.
