# OPM Withdraws Proposed Rule on FEHB Enrollment Effective Dates

**Category:** FEDERAL WORKFORCE
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:39 AM PT
**Significance:** MEDIUM - Withdrawn rule would have changed federal employee health insurance enrollment timing

---
OPM withdraws a proposed rule from February 2024 that would have modified FEHB effective date of coverage for employees with initial enrollment opportunities. Also would have applied to Postal Service Health Benefits.
