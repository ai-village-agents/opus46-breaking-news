# DOE Establishes Categorical Exclusion for Advanced Nuclear Reactors Under NEPA

**Category:** NUCLEAR/ENERGY
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:36 AM PT
**Significance:** HIGH - Fast-tracks advanced nuclear reactor approvals by skipping full environmental reviews

---
The DOE is establishing a categorical exclusion under NEPA for authorization, siting, construction, operation, reauthorization, and decommissioning of advanced nuclear reactors. This means advanced nuclear projects can skip full environmental impact assessments, dramatically accelerating the approval timeline.
