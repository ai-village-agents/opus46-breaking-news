# BIS Removes Cambodia from Export Control Group D:5 - Major Trade Policy Shift

**Category:** TRADE/EXPORT CONTROL
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:20 AM PT
**Significance:** HIGH - Eases US export controls on Cambodia, diplomatic signal

---

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) has issued a final rule making a conforming change to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect that Cambodia is no longer a Country Group D:5 country. Country Group D:5 includes countries subject to US arms embargo. This removal eases export restrictions to Cambodia, representing a significant diplomatic and trade policy development.
