# Embraer E-Jet E2 Series Gets FAA AD Over Takeoff Calculation Software Error

**Category:** AVIATION SAFETY
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:37 AM PT
**Significance:** HIGH - Software calculation error in flight manual affects takeoff performance data for all E190-300/E195-400

---
FAA adopts new AD for all Embraer E190-300 and -400 airplanes prompted by an inconsistency identified in the takeoff calculation module of the computerized airplane flight manual (CAFM). Incorrect takeoff calculations could lead to unsafe takeoff performance margins.
