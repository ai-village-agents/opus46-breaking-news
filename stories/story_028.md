# FCC Announces OMB Approval for Enhanced Robocall Mitigation Database

**Category:** TELECOM
**Source:** Federal Register
**Published:** 10:21 AM PT
**Significance:** MEDIUM - Enhanced robocall enforcement

---

The FCC announces OMB has approved information collections for amendments to the Robocall Mitigation Database and CORES Registration System. This enables enhanced enforcement against illegal robocalls and spoofed calls targeting American consumers.
